## What are Absence Notifications?

Absence notifications allow your support staff to submit notifications digitally indicating they will not be in for work on a specific day (in most cases the day of submission), this triggers this absence to flow through to SupportWise support data and determine impact, so you can jump in and easily re allocate as part of the daily operations flow.

## Enabling Absence Notifcations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Manage Settings Page">
    Simply click [here](https://supportwise.app/configuration/settings)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Under Workspace Features">
    Enable **Staff absence notifications**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Support staff can now submit absence notifications">
    <Check>
      All done - learn more about features relating to Absence Notifcations
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
